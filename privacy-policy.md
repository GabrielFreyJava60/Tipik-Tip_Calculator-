## How the App Works

Liquid Tip Calculator is a **fully offline** application:

- ✅ All calculations are performed locally on your device
- ✅ No data is sent to external servers
- ✅ No internet connection is required
- ✅ Your tip calculations remain private on your device
- ✅ Language and currency preferences are stored locally only

## Data Storage

Any preferences you set (language, currency) are stored only on your device using local storage. This data:

- Remains on your device only
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Is not accessible to us or third parties
- Is deleted if you uninstall the app
